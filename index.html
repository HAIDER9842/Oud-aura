Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including name, email address, shipping address, and payment details when you place an order. We may also collect browsing data to improve your experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and fulfill your orders
  • To send order confirmations and shipping updates
  • To respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • To send promotional communications (with your consent)
  • To improve our website and services

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties without your consent, except trusted partners who assist in operating our website or servicing you, as long as those parties agree to keep this information confidential.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information. All payment transactions are encrypted using SSL technology.

5. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enhance your experience, analyze site traffic, and personalize content. You may disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may affect site functionality.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@oudaura.com.

7. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: privacy@oudaura.com
